Cocido Madrileño

Description

The Spain's capital cooked symbol. Everyone in Madrid loves this energy food.

Traditionaly served in three steps. First the soup, second vegetables and chickpeas and third the meat.

Ingredients (for 4)

  • Chickpeas - 200 gr
  • Veal's knee bone - 1 unit
  • Pig's spine bone - 2 units
  • Black pudding - 300 gr
  • Chicken - 200 gr
  • Tocino - 100 gr
  • Fresh chorizo - 2 units
  • Potatoes - 2 units
  • Carrots - 3 units
  • Cabbage - 1/2 unit
  • Noodles - 80 gr
  • Olive oil
  • Salt
  • Garlic - 2 cloves

Steps

  1. The night before cooking, put the Chickpeas in water with two salt spoons.
  2. Go with the cooking. Put the bones, the black pudding, the Chicken and the Tocino in a cooking pot with water until it boils. Then take off the formed foam and put the chickpeas into the cooking pot.
  3. Cook all for three hours or until the chickpeas are soft.
  4. In the meantime, put the Cabbage in another pot and cook it for thirty minutes. Then drain it, cut it and fry it in a pan with the garlickand some salt.
  5. After that, we need to cook the fresh chorizo in another pot for five minutes.
  6. Cut the Carrots and the Potatoes and add them to the big pot thirty minutes left.
  7. Add the Noodles two minutes left.
  8. Enjoy!
